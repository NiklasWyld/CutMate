CutMate

CutMate keeps track of your copied items, allowing you to easily access and paste previously copied content. It enhances productivity by providing a convenient way to revisit and reuse your clipboard contents.

Download Latest

A new type of clipboard

With CutMate, you don't just have one object on the clipboard, but several, this makes your workflow much easier and faster.

Do it the way you want it

You can customize the entire look of CutMate to suit you perfectly.

Search feature

Find the copy you are looking for within a few milliseconds.

CutMate is more than just text

You can easily copy not only text but also images and files
